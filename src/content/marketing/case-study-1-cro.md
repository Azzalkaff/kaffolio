## Problem Framing
The client, a mid-sized e-commerce brand, experienced a high cart abandonment rate (over 70%). Users were enthusiastically adding items to their carts but dropping off abruptly during the payment step, leading to a massive bottleneck in the revenue pipeline.

## Objective & KPI
- **Primary KPI:** Increase successful checkout completion rate.
- **Secondary KPI:** Decrease cart abandonment at the payment gateway step.
- **Goal:** Achieve a frictionless buying experience without sacrificing necessary user data.

## Strategy
Instead of blindly redesigning, we focused on **friction-reduction**:
- **Behavioral Analysis:** Mapped out user drop-off points using Hotjar recordings.
- **Cognitive Load Reduction:** Eliminated the mandatory "Create Account" barrier.
- **Trust Injection:** Added security signals right beside the credit card input fields.

## Execution
1. **CRO Audit:** Discovered that 60% of mobile users abandoned the cart when asked for a password.
2. **A/B Testing:** Implemented a single-page React checkout flow with a "Guest Checkout" option.
3. **Redesign:** Streamlined the form fields, grouping shipping and billing into logical, collapsible steps.
4. **Trust Signals:** Integrated Norton Secured badges and clear 30-day return policies directly below the 'Pay' button.

## Learnings
The biggest insight was that **speed beats data collection**. By forcing users to create an account to "build an email list", the client was actually losing immediate revenue. Offering Guest Checkout captured the sale first, and we later converted 40% of those guests into registered accounts post-purchase via a specialized thank-you page flow.
