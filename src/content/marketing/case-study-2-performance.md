## Problem Framing
A new fashion startup wanted to scale their customer acquisition during the highly competitive Q4 holiday season. However, they were struggling with a bloated Cost Per Acquisition (CPA) on Facebook and Instagram Ads. Their creative assets were visually stunning, but flawed targeting and broken pixel tracking were bleeding their budget dry.

## Objective & KPI
- **Primary KPI:** Achieve a minimum 4x Return on Ad Spend (ROAS).
- **Secondary KPI:** Reduce blended CPA by at least 30%.
- **Goal:** Scale ad spend profitably without hitting ad fatigue.

## Strategy
We deployed a **Full-Funnel Segmentation Strategy**:
- **Data Integrity:** Rebuilding the tracking foundation to bypass iOS 14+ restrictions.
- **Audience Segmentation:** Isolating Top of Funnel (Brand Awareness), Middle of Funnel (Traffic/Consideration), and Bottom of Funnel (Conversion/Retargeting).
- **Creative Fatigue Prevention:** Dynamic creative testing matrices.

## Execution
1. **Pixel Restructuring:** Implemented Meta Conversions API (CAPI) server-side via Shopify to ensure 99% data match rates.
2. **Top of Funnel:** Ran broad video views campaigns focusing on brand storytelling.
3. **Bottom of Funnel:** Deployed Dynamic Product Ads (DPA) targeting cart abandoners with personalized discount codes.
4. **A/B Testing:** Tested 5 different primary text variations and 3 headline angles every week to find the winning combination.

## Learnings
Beautiful creatives mean nothing if the tracking is broken. Once the CAPI was properly installed, the algorithm had enough high-quality signals to find buyers efficiently. Additionally, separating the retargeting budget strictly to 20% of the total spend prevented audience exhaustion while maximizing the harvest of high-intent buyers.
